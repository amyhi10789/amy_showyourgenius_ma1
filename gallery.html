Here is a gallery with different k-pop groups. According to general consensus by fans, the below groups are the 5 most famous k-pop groups in the world. These groups have achieved massive international success, have huge fanbases, and have sold millions of albums, solidiying their status as K-pop music powerhouses. Enjoy!

BTS (k-pop boy group under HYBE)
find BTS' instagram page!

BTS are considered the most famous k-pop boy group, and known for being globally influencial, as well as producing their own music. They are a 7-member group under HYBE Entertainment.

BLACKPINK (k-pop girl group under YG)
find BLACKPINK's instagram page!

BLACKPINK are considered the most famous k-pop girl group, and known for their "girl crush" aesthetic. They are a 4-member group under YG Entertainment.

Stray Kids (k-pop boy group under JYPE)
find Stray Kids' instagram page!

Stray Kids are one of the most famous k-pop groups in the 4th gen of k-pop, and known for their flashy beats, as well as producing their own music. They are an 8-member group under JYP Entertainment.

TWICE (k-pop girl group under JYPE)
find TWICE's instagram page!

TWICE are also extremely renowned, known for their colorful, bright songs, and eye-catching choreography. They are a 9-member group under JYP Entertainment.

SEVENTEEN (k-pop boy group under HYBE)
find SEVENTEEN's instagram page!

SEVENTEEN are popular as well, known for their powerful yet synchronized choreographies, and the fact that their name is SEVENTEEN even though they have 13 members. They are a 13-member group under HYBE Entertainment.
